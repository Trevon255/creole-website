Our Story

Creole is born from the soul of a natural creative. What started as a quiet evening hobby soon blossomed into a vibrant business dedicated to the celebration of Jamaican craftsmanship.

The true spark for Creole was ignited in the skies. While serving as an air hostess with Caribbean Airlines, our founder traversed the islands and the world, gaining a unique perspective on global aesthetics while carrying the warmth of Jamaica in her heart.

Today, Creole stands as a bridge between traditional island techniques and modern luxury decor transforming simple clay and fiber into timeless statements of artistry.
